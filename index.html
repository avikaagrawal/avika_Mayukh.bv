About Us

Mayukh

Mayukh is the grand annual technical fest of Banasthali Vidyapith — a celebration of creativity, technology, and innovation. It brings together bright minds from all years of B.Tech to collaborate, compete, and explore futuristic ideas through hackathons, technical challenges, and project showcases. With a spirit of discovery and teamwork, Mayukh 2026 aims to illuminate the path toward a smarter and more connected tomorrow.
